Who Are We

Presently, the P4P team consists of five high school students from Upper Canada College in Toronto, Canada. While we may be young, we each share a passion and spirit for entrepreneurship and charitable initiatives to support those around us. Despite being confined to our homes, we recognize that tough times never last, but tough people do. We are excited to start this journey together.
